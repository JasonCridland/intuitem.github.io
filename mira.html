Mira vision

As cyber security practitioners, we have tested so many risk management approaches and tools. As they grow, they have drifted away from what matters: users journeys and their productivity:

Security Teams

Streamlined workflow with reuse capabilities and coherence analysis to help with analysis quality.

Development and Operations Teams

Automatic ticket creation and filling, reminders and extract of remediation plan.

Organization management

Multiple reporting and analytics capabilities to map risk exposure and help with decision making.
